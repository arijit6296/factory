About us

Welcome to Repute Steel & Engineering Company!

Our mission is to become one of the most trusted manufacturers and exporters of ferrous and non-ferrous metals in the world, and we achieve this through our commitment to excellence in every aspect of our operations.

At RSEC, we take pride in our technologically advanced infrastructure, which enables us to work effectively towards our objectives. Our team of experienced visionaries works together towards creating innovative solutions that contribute to the sustainable development of the world.

We are manufacturer,stockists and suppliers of products such as Flanges, Pipe Fittings, Fasteners, Pipes, Tubes, Rods, Bars, Sheets, Plates, Coils, Valves, Flats, and

more to various industries globally, including Oil and Gas, Shipbuilding, Pharmaceuticals, Nuclear & Energy, Food Processing, Chemical, Fertilizer, Construction, Automobiles, Solar, and others.

We excel in manufacturing and exporting premium quality Fittings, Fasteners & Flanges in Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Stainless Steel, Duplex Stainless Steel, and all types of alloy materials in ferrous and non-ferrous metals. When it comes to the best quality, RSEC is a most referred manufacturer and exporter in the flanges, fittings, fasteners, pipes, tubes, electrical products, and earthing products.

We can help you import any material/product strictly adhering to your preference & quality constraints. We can arrange for chemical, physical, mechanical, ultrasonic, micro, IGC, and any other related test reports from government-approved laboratories. We do comply with any third-party inspection of our material/product if needed.

We have grown rapidly through our excellent working staff and technological upgradations at regular intervals. We maintain a friendly, supportive work culture, where quality is our top priority.

With a growing presence in multiple regions around the world, we're excited to continue expanding our offerings. We are dedicated to providing the best quality products and services to our valued clients.
